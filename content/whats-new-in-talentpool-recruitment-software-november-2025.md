Most recruiters don't struggle because of a lack of tools.

They struggle because they spend too much time on repetitive, manual work like attaching documents, re-entering candidate data, and fixing gaps between systems.

Industry research consistently shows that recruiters spend less than half their time on actual candidate engagement, with the rest going into administrative tasks. This is one of the biggest reasons hiring slows down and [candidate drop-off](https://www.thetalentpool.ai/blogs/how-to-reduce-candidate-drop-offs), even when teams are using modern recruitment software.

Our November feature release focuses on solving this exact problem. Let's dive into what's new and how these updates help your team move faster with less effort.

## **Update 1 - Employee Referrals - Custom Forms and Auto Updates**

Recruiters frequently juggle repetitive data entry every time someone refers a candidate. Our update makes this effortless.

### **What changed in the November updates?**

- **Build your own referral form:** Add custom fields that match your process and data collection needs.
- **Skip repetitive data entry task:** Candidate data entered in the employee referral form automatically enters the Basic Info of the candidate within Talent Pool.
- **Export referral details instantly:** Get clean, structured data for reporting or audits with one click.

### **How does this help recruiters?**

- Eliminates repetitive data entry
- Offers easy customization
- Removes the need for copy-pasting details from forms or emails, or sheets
- Reduces data errors and incomplete profiles
- Enables faster referral screening and shortlisting

Referrals become simpler to control and can be turned into interviews more quickly since recruiters can take action on them at any given moment.

## **Update 2 - Auto-Attach Job Descriptions**

Manual email editing is a hidden bottleneck in talent workflows.

### **What changed in the November updates?**

Now, you can enable the JD attachment functionality to get rid of this manual task. Each time you send an interview or appointment mail using Talentpool [recruitment software](https://www.thetalentpool.ai/), the right Job Description is automatically attached to the mail.

### **How does this help recruiters?**

- Eliminates the need to manually attach or resend JDs
- Reduces candidate queries before interviews
- Ensures candidates come prepared and aligned with hiring managers

Recruiters save time on all interviews scheduled and enhance interview preparedness and consistency without incurring any additional processes.
