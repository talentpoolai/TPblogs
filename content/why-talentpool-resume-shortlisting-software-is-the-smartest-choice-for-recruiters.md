## **What is a Resume Screening Software?**

Resume screening software is a specialized capability of [recruitment software](https://www.thetalentpool.ai/blogs/the-ultimate-guide-to-selecting-the-right-recruitment-software) that specifically aims at screening resumes in relation to job requirements. The resume screening feature also actively analyzes the resumes, unlike simpler ATS systems that merely maintain them.

This program operates on the basis of parsing, keyword analysis, and artificial intelligence-based logic to determine the degree to which a candidate fits a position. More advanced [automated resume screening](https://www.thetalentpool.ai/blogs/future-trends-in-automated-resume-screening) software takes it a step further and not only looks at keywords but also looks at context, so that strong candidates are not overlooked by recruiters because of differences in formatting or wording.

**Key functions of CV screening software include:**

- Extracting skills, job titles, experience, education, and location from resumes
- Matching candidate profiles to job descriptions automatically
- Ranking candidates based on relevance and fit
- Reducing bias through consistent evaluation criteria
- Enabling recruiters to shortlist candidates faster and more accurately

For high-volume hiring teams, automated resume screening software is no longer optional. It is essential for maintaining speed, accuracy, and fairness in early-stage recruitment.

## **How is Resume Shortlisting Done?**

The majority of recruiters are aware of the [AI resume screening software](https://www.thetalentpool.ai/blogs/the-future-of-hiring-will-ai-resume-screening-software-replace-recruiters), and its effects become more apparent when you learn how it is used.

### **Parsing and Structuring Resumes**

The resume management system extracts skills, education, and experience and places them in structured fields, which can be filtered or searched by the recruiters.

### **Identifying Duplicates**

Modern resume screening software checks resumes for duplicates during import, so recruiters avoid reviewing the same candidate twice.

### **Ranking Candidates with AI**

AI assesses the fit of a candidate to a position based on specifications (e.g., skill sets, years of experience, and location proximity). The best AI-based CV screening software will showcase the most promising profiles on top so that the recruitment team can pay attention to the best candidates first.

### **Shortlisting Automatically**

Rather than going through hundreds of resumes manually to know about the applicants, recruiters get an AI-filtered shortlist that matches the requirements of a position.

### **Recruiter Review and Final Selection**

Recruiters confirm AI suggestions, exercise human judgment, and proceed with shortlisted candidates through interviews.

By using resume shortlisting software, teams reduce screening time dramatically while improving consistency and hiring accuracy.

### **Continuous Learning**

Smart systems improve accuracy over time based on recruiter feedback and hiring decisions.

Together, these capabilities turn resume screening into a high-speed, high-accuracy process that is consistent for every role.
